This example demonstrates the drag & drop functionality of the ezeep.js component. Users can drag files directly into the upload area or select them by clicking.

How it Works

  • Renders a drag & drop upload area
  • Users can drag files into it or select them by clicking
  • No fileurl required - files are uploaded from the user's device
  • Print dialog opens automatically after upload
  • Supports various file formats (PDF, images, documents, etc.)

Attributes Used

  • clientid: Your registered Client-ID
  • redirecturi: Your registered Redirect URI
  • trigger="file": Renders the drag & drop area
  • theme: Color scheme (e.g. "blue", "green", "pink")
  • appearance: "light", "dark" or "system"
